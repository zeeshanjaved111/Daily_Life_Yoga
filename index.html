The Story

From Healing to Teaching

My yoga journey began not in a studio but in a doctor's office, with a diagnosis of severe postural issues and chronic stress that medications alone couldn't fix. Yoga was my last resort — and it changed everything.

Over eight years, I immersed myself in study across multiple disciplines: therapeutic yoga, pranayama, anatomy, and the ancient philosophical traditions behind the practice. What I discovered wasn't just physical relief — it was a complete reimagining of how to live.

Today, Daily Yoga exists to give you what took me years to learn — with clarity, compassion, and curriculum that truly works. I've guided students through back pain, anxiety, hormonal imbalances, and athletic recovery, always with one goal: lasting transformation, not temporary fixes.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior yoga experience to join the masterclass?

+

No experience is necessary. The 20-hour masterclass is designed to be accessible to complete beginners while offering enough depth to challenge intermediate practitioners. We adapt the content to your level throughout the program.

Is online yoga consultancy as effective as in-person?

+

Yes — our one-to-one consultancy sessions are conducted via video and are equally effective. You'll receive real-time corrections, personalized feedback, and the same level of attention as an in-person session.

Can yoga help with medical conditions like thyroid or diabetes?

+

Therapeutic yoga for diabetes and thyroid conditions has strong research support. Our therapeutic programs are designed to complement medical treatment — always in consultation with your doctor. Many students see meaningful improvements in managing their conditions through consistent practice.

Is prenatal yoga safe in all trimesters?

+

Yes, with proper guidance. Our prenatal program is trimester-specific, with modifications for each stage. We always recommend consulting your OB-GYN before beginning any exercise program during pregnancy, and we work in alignment with their guidance.

How quickly will I see results?

+

Most students notice improvements in sleep, mood, and physical tension within the first 2 weeks. Deeper physical changes like improved flexibility, posture, and pain reduction typically become pronounced within 4-6 weeks of consistent practice.

What equipment do I need?

+

Just a yoga mat and comfortable clothing. We'll recommend specific props (blocks, straps, bolsters) only if they're genuinely helpful for your practice — and often household items work perfectly as substitutes.
